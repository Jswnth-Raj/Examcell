About Examination Cell

Teaching and evaluation are the two sides of a coin. Examination cell plays a key role in evaluation part. The Examination Cell (Exam Cell) in GIITS was headed by the Chief Superintendent of Examinations ( Principal ) with supporting staff. The Prime responsibility of Examination Cell is conducting of all examinations (Both Internal and External Exams) in fair and systematic manner under the directions
of the Chief Superintendent of Examinations.
 

Primary Functions of Examination Cell

1.Examination Cell serves all Examination notices received from University to all concerned. 2.Examination cell prepares Circulars for students regarding Exam Fee Collection, the last date of fee Collection, modalities of payments of fine etc.
3.Examination Cell takes all precautions while preparing Examination Time table, Invigilation duty chart, seating plans for the students in the Examination halls, smooth conduct of Examinations etc.
4.Examination Cell mobilizes the proper staff during the Examination time, and assigning them duty as per the duty chart already prepared.
